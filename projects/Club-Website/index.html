Who we are

Welcome to Outside the Binary, a vibrant community group for non-binary individuals in St. Pete, Florida. Founded by Prin, this group was born from a need in the community and their own experiences. At OTB, we come together to socialize, share our stories, and create meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. Our two monthly meetings, both virtual and in-person, and active Discord channel keep us connected and help us build new memories and friendships. Join us and be a part of this supportive and inclusive community.
